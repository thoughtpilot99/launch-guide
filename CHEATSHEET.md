# Launch Post Cheatsheet

One page. Print it. Pin it.

---

## The 5 Beats

```
1. ANNOUNCE   →  "Introducing [Product], the [world's first] [Category]."
2. PROBLEM    →  One sentence + knife-twisting adjective ("brutally slow")
3. SOLUTION   →  One sentence + quality guard ("without [bad thing]")
4. HOW        →  3-4 steps in arrows (→) or numbers
5. CTA        →  Engagement ("Comment X for Y") OR link (in REPLY, never main post)
```

---

## The 8 Hard Rules

1. ❌ **No link in the main post** — algorithm suppression. Link goes in reply.
2. 👤 **Founder account, not company account** — 2.75x impressions, 14x engagement.
3. ⏰ **Tue / Wed / Thu, 9am PT** — overlaps EU + EC + WC.
4. 🔥 **Warm the account 3-5 days prior** — 15-20 min/day of real engagement.
5. ⚡ **Engagement sprint: 15 min before + 2 hrs after** — reply thoughtfully to every comment.
6. 🔁 **Team reposts from their own accounts** — brand account amplifies, doesn't lead.
7. 🚫 **No em dashes** — AI-tell. Use periods + line breaks.
8. 🎯 **Post hook must match video hook** — same energy, same first words.

## The 6 Archetypes

| # | Archetype | Length | Use when |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | YC single-tweet | 1 tweet + reply | Product self-explains; founder has audience |
| 2 | Origin thread | 8-12 tweets / long post | Story > feature; first-time founder; unusual journey |
| 3 | Teardown / reveal | 10-15 tweets | "How we built it" is the flex |
| 4 | Fundraise | Long post (5-13 tweets) | Raise + full origin story |
| 5 | Lead-magnet + upsell | Single tweet | Free resource funnels to paid service |
| 6 | Agency amplification | 1-3 tweets | Writing about a client's launch |

## The 12 Hook Patterns

```
1. Numbered-proof          "I analyzed [N] [things]. [Bold claim]."
2. Distance/constraint     Specific number + unlikely constraint
3. AI-shock                "I made this with [N] prompts. Creatives are cooked."
4. Inversion               "[X] will [Y]" — flip the expected relationship
5. Brutal-truth            Flat indictment, present tense, no softener
6. Side-by-side            "Two [things]. The difference is [Nx]."
7. Personal-journey        Naive past self + "Spoiler:" line
8. Cohort-analysis         "I analyzed all [N] [cohort]. [N] traits separate winners."
9. Unbelievable-claim      A number that sounds fake. Tweet 2 proves it.
10. Result-first           "[Client] did [absurd metric]. Here's [the asset]."
11. Question               Pointed question the post answers
12. "Today we're launching" Direct, no games — only with audience
```

## CTA Ladder (Pick ONE)

```
SOFT  ────────────────────────────────────────────────  HARD
Brand thesis → Inclusive invite → Founder DM → Multi-CTA → Comment-gated → Contest → Direct link
```

**Best by archetype:**
- YC single-tweet: Direct link in REPLY
- Origin thread: Brand thesis OR founder DM
- Fundraise: Brand thesis only
- Lead-magnet: Comment-gated OR direct link
- Agency amplification: Founder DM ("DM to book")

## The Hook-Match Rule

```
Video hook:  "50 commits beats five commits, right?"
Post hook:   "50 commits. Or 5. Which actually matters?"
                     ✅ MATCH

Video hook:  "I built this in 6 prompts."
Post hook:   "🚀 Excited to launch our new product."
                     ❌ DOES NOT MATCH (Bilanc problem)
```

---

## Anti-Patterns (Do Not Ship)

- ❌ "Today, we're excited to share..."
- ❌ "Stay tuned" / "Big news coming"
- ❌ Stacked CTAs (sign up + follow + newsletter + Discord)
- ❌ Link in main post
- ❌ "Watch the video 👇"
- ❌ Em dashes anywhere
- ❌ "It's not X, it's Y" (AI-tell)
- ❌ "As a founder, I..." (only works for Anton Osika because he has the story)
- ❌ Stacked superlatives ("most powerful, most robust, most comprehensive")
- ❌ Posting from company account on launch day

## Quick Decision Tree

```
Have a viral demo video?  ──→ AI-era single tweet (Bolt/Devin style)
Got an unusual origin?    ──→ Origin thread or fundraise long-post
Just raised a round?      ──→ Fundraise long-post (Lovable/Deel style)
Giving away a free tool?  ──→ Lead-magnet + service upsell (Icon style)
Writing for a client?     ──→ Agency amplification (Represent style)
None of the above?        ──→ YC-style single tweet (default)
```

## The Master Sentence Templates

Steal these. Customize the noun:

- **Announcement:** `Introducing [Product], the [world's first] [Category].`
- **Analogy bridge:** `[Product] is like [Familiar A] + [Familiar B], but for [new use-case].`
- **Quality guard:** `Get [N]x more [outcome] without losing [quality].`
- **Lovable-template:** `[Company] is the fastest growing startup in [region] ever, $[X] ARR in [Y] [time].`
- **Brand thesis closer:** `This is the age of the [X].` / `All good things start small and shaky.`

## The Pre-Ship Checklist

```
[ ] First 10 words contain product name + category
[ ] Problem statement has knife-twisting adjective
[ ] Solution has quality guard
[ ] How-it-works is 3-4 terse steps in → or numbers
[ ] ONE CTA (not stacked)
[ ] Link in REPLY, not main post
[ ] No em dashes anywhere
[ ] No "excited to share" / "stay tuned"
[ ] Founder account (verified)
[ ] Posted Tue/Wed/Thu 9am PT
[ ] Account warmed 3-5 days prior
[ ] Team pinged to repost
[ ] First 2 hours blocked for engagement sprint
[ ] Video hook matches post hook (if video)
```
