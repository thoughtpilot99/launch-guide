# Lovable — "$200M Series A" + Origin Story

## Verbatim post

> Today Lovable raised $200M at a $1.8B valuation led by Accel.
>
> I've never shared how it started: I called my friend at 6AM to go for a walk...
>
> I was CTO at Depict. We hit $1M ARR faster than any Swedish startup people heard of. But after 3 years, something felt off. E-commerce optimization wasn't how I wanted to spend my life. I kept thinking I want to do something better.
>
> In 2023, after ChatGPT 3 had just released, I started exploring what complex systems you could build with AI. One weekend, I hacked together something simple: 'plain english to working app.' I called it GPT Engineer. Posted it on GitHub. Went to sleep.
>
> I woke up to chaos. Over the next few weeks it got hundreds of thousands of users. 50k+ GitHub stars in weeks. My inbox was destroyed. But the messages weren't from developers. They were from people who'd never coded before, building their first apps. That's when it clicked.
>
> The real insight: 99% of the world's best ideas are trapped in the heads of people who can't code. They have problems. They know the solutions. They just can't build them. AI shouldn't help engineers code faster. It should help everyone else build.
>
> 6AM. I called Fabian: 'Let's go for a walk, I have an idea.' 'When?' 'I'm outside.' He came down in slippers 10 minutes later. We walked through Stockholm for 2 hours. By 8AM, we'd decided to start a company together. Not to make coding easier. To make it obsolete.
>
> We're not trying to 'capture' the existing market for building apps and websites. We're creating a new market. Let me explain: Before Canva, there were only 5M 'designers' that used Photoshop. Canva made everyone capable of designing, 44x'd the market to 220M designers. Lovable is doing the same for code and 'producing' 1 billion builders by building the product that builds all other products.
>
> Today, there are 47M developers worldwide. Lovable is going to produce 1B potential builders.
>
> 8 months in we're already seeing non-technical founders build successful software businesses with Lovable: Caio Moretti -> made $3M in revenue in 48 hours with a product built on Lovable. Airweave (agentic search tool for internal databases) -> recently secured admission to Y Combinator Felipe -> hit $10k MRR with his laundry subscription product.
>
> Building software takes 300+ hours. But when you can spin up a functional app in <1 hour, you can make builders out of everyone. Lovable already has 2.3M users building.
>
> Huge shoutout to the team, users, and investors. We're not just democratizing code. We're helping everyone realize they have things to build.
