## Pattern analysis

**Hook:** "After months of building in stealth, I'm excited to introduce Trava, the AI compliance engine built for importers."

**Structural beats:**
1. Stealth-reveal opener
2. Market pain stat ($200B duties — memorable, big, specific)
3. 3-part pain list (brokers overloaded / tariffs shift / liability)
4. Product reframe ("Trava changes this")
5. Traction proof ("first audits... significant refunds in days")
6. Vision frame ("AI infrastructure for trade compliance")
7. Two-tier CTA (DM or waitlist)

**Why it works:** The $200B stat is the workhorse. "Stealth → reveal" is a clean frame when there's no funding announcement. The "early access, DM me" CTA is low-commitment and puts the founder in the sales loop.

**Lessons for the skill:**
- When no fundraise to announce: "stealth + market stat + personal CTA" is the cleanest launch-post skeleton.
- "DM me if [specific role]" outperforms "Sign up here" for enterprise / high-consideration products.
- Vision paragraph ("Long term, we're building...") is optional but earns repeat shares.
