## How to use this index

1. Pick a launch that matches YOUR situation (similar product type, similar audience size, similar moment).
2. Click through and read the actual post on its native platform — see the formatting, the comments, the engagement pattern.
3. Open the corresponding [corpus file](corpus/INDEX.md) for the verbatim text + pattern analysis.
4. Steal the structure (not the words).
