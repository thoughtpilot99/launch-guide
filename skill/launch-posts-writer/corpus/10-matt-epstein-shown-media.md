## Epstein DNA (distilled for the skill)

1. **Lead with a receipt number.** 50M views / 90 days / 39 people / 15→58. First 10 words always have a number.
2. **Pair receipt with curiosity-gap or absurd contrast.** Mom can't use iPhone. "Disturbing." "F*cked part."
3. **Name the framework so readers can repeat it.** The Mom Test. Nuclear launch. The Sorority Play.
4. **Add "(steal it)" or similar.** Weaponizes generosity as distribution.
5. **Drop named enemies or proximity.** Shopify, Gymshark, Triple Whale founder's Shabbat, Whop office.
6. **End on a flex or stealable list — never a CTA.**

**The upsell mechanic:** every "here's what I/we did" post doubles as a case study. The DM asking "can you do this for us?" is the conversion. He never pitches in-post. The flex IS the pitch.

**Adapt-for-founder version:** Founders can use the same structure by naming their investors (proximity), their competitor (named enemy), and their framework. The "(steal it)" generosity move works just as well for a founder giving away their prompt library as for an agency giving away their launch playbook.
