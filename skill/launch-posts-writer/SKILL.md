# Launch Posts Writer

Write SaaS product launch posts and threads. The launch video is the anchor asset; the post is the **distribution wrapper** that sets up the watch, frames the moment, and converts attention into the next action (try / signup / waitlist / discovery call / lead magnet).

---

## Start here — the 4 intake questions

Before drafting, nail down:

1. **Who is the author?** Founder's account? Ghostwritten? Agency amplifying a client?
2. **What's the ask?** Product signup / waitlist / discovery call / brand awareness / free resource?
3. **What's the video?** Duration + hook line + what it shows. The post's hook should MATCH the video's hook.
4. **Archetype?** Pick one: (1) single-tweet announcement, (2) origin thread, (3) teardown/reveal, (4) fundraise, (5) lead-magnet + upsell, (6) agency amplification. See `references/01-format-taxonomy.md`.

If any of these four are unclear, ask before drafting.

---

## The spine — Renat's 5-beat formula

Every launch post maps to these five beats. See `references/06-renat-5-beat-formula.md` for the full canonical version.

1. **Clear announcement** — `Introducing [Product], the [world's first / best] [Category].`
2. **Problem statement** — one sentence + adjective that "twists the knife"
3. **Solution** — one sentence + quality guard ("without [bad thing]")
4. **How it works** — 3-4 steps in arrows or numbers
5. **CTA** — engagement tactic ("Comment X for Y") OR direct link (in reply, never main post)

---

## Hard rules (non-negotiable)

1. **NEVER put the link in the main post.** X and LinkedIn both suppress external links. Put link in reply tweet or comment.
2. **Post from FOUNDER's account**, not company account. 2.75x impressions, 5x engagement, 14x vs brand account.
3. **Timing: Tue/Wed/Thu, 9am PT.** Overlaps EU + East Coast + West Coast.
4. **Warm the account 3-5 days before launch.** 15-20 min/day of real engagement on posts from people you know.
5. **Engagement sprint:** 15 min before publish + 2 hours after. Respond to every comment.
6. **Team reposts.** Company account amplifies; doesn't publish original launch.
7. **NO em dashes.** (User preference — AI-tell.) Use periods + line breaks.
8. **Match the video hook.** If the video opens with a question, the post opens with the same question (or tighter).

## Workflow

### Step 1 — Pick the archetype
Read `references/01-format-taxonomy.md`. Match the situation to one of 6 archetypes. Don't blend archetypes.

### Step 2 — Pick the length
Read `references/03-length-format-decision.md`. Pick: single tweet / long tweet / numbered thread. Default by archetype:
- YC announcement → single tweet
- Origin story → thread (8-12)
- Teardown/reveal → thread (10-15)
- Fundraise → thread (5-8) or long post (2025+ default)
- Lead-magnet → single tweet
- Agency amplification → single tweet OR mini-thread (2-3)

### Step 3 — Draft the hook (first 7 words)
Read `references/02-hook-patterns.md`. Pick one of 12 archetypes. Test the hook by reading it aloud.

### Step 4 — Fill the 5 beats
Use `references/06-renat-5-beat-formula.md` as the template. Draft each beat in one sentence. Tighten after.

### Step 5 — Pick the CTA
Read `references/05-cta-ladder.md`. Pick ONE CTA. Do NOT stack.

### Step 6 — Wrap the video
Read `references/04-video-integration.md`. Match post hook to video hook. Confirm embed placement.

### Step 7 — Pre-ship check
Run the checklist at the bottom of `references/06-renat-5-beat-formula.md`.

## Voice variants

**Founder voice (default):** Personal "I" pronouns. Origin detail. Specific nickname / city / time. First-person plural for co-founded companies.

**Ghostwriter voice:** Write AS the founder. Match their existing posts' cadence, emoji usage, capitalization, signature phrases. Read 5+ of their posts before drafting.

**Agency-amplification voice:** Result-first ("[Client] did [absurd metric]. Here's the 60-second video."). Result IS the pitch. Service-plug goes in LAST SENTENCE or reply, not middle.

## The upsell/lead-magnet variant

When the post is the top of funnel for an agency or free resource that funnels to a service:
- Lead with the free resource (Icon's 1,000 winning ads; Renat's Launch GPT).
- Show its value in 1-2 sentences.
- **Service plug in the LAST SENTENCE of the post**, never the middle. Example: "We take 3 launch slots per quarter. If yours is coming up, DM."
- The reader who wants the free thing AND wants your service has committed to reading. A service line at the end reads as a soft "by the way." A service line in the middle reads as salesy.

See `corpus/11-icon-kennan-davison.md` (the rosetta stone) and `corpus/18-renat-primary-formula.md` (Renat's own video — which itself is an example of this pattern).

## Anti-patterns (do not ship)

- ❌ "Today, we're excited to share..." (throat-clear)
- ❌ "Stay tuned" / "Big news coming" (vague promise)
- ❌ Stacked CTAs ("Sign up + follow + newsletter + Discord")
- ❌ Link in main post (suppressed by algorithms)
- ❌ Watch the video 👇 (redundant if embedded, ignored if external)
- ❌ Em dashes ("—") — use periods + line breaks
- ❌ "It's not X, it's Y" symmetric rhetoric (AI-tell)
- ❌ "As a founder, I..." — works for Anton Osika because he has the story. For anyone else it sounds AI-generated.
- ❌ Launching from company account instead of founder account
- ❌ Stacking superlatives ("most powerful, most robust, most comprehensive")
