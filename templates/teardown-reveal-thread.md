# Template — Teardown / Reveal Thread

**Use for:** Technically impressive builds. AI-era launches where "how we built it" is as interesting as "what we built." Process IS the flex.

**Length:** 10-15 tweets. Reveal video mid-thread.

**Real anchors:** PJ Ace AI-ad drops, Devin launch thread, Cursor dev-tool reveals, Matt Epstein "I made this with 10 prompts" genre.

---

## Fill-in skeleton (12-tweet version)

```
Tweet 1 (IMPOSSIBLE-SOUNDING HOOK):
We built [THING] in [ABSURD TIME] with [ABSURD CONSTRAINT].

[Optional: thumbnail of the final result — enough to tease, not enough to spoil]

Tweet 2 (STAKES):
[Why this matters. Why people said it couldn't be done. The naysayer.]

Tweet 3 (BEFORE):
[What this used to take. Time, money, team size. The old way.]

Tweet 4 (THE INSIGHT):
[The key realization. One sentence. The thing you saw that others missed.]

Tweet 5 (BUILD STEP 1):
[Screenshot/code/diagram. One decision explained.]

Tweet 6 (BUILD STEP 2):
[Another decision. Include the failure you hit and how you got past it.]

Tweet 7 (BUILD STEP 3):
[A surprise. Something that worked better than expected.]

Tweet 8 (REVEAL — video/demo drops here):
Here's the final result:

[VIDEO EMBEDDED — this is the payoff moment]

Tweet 9 (NUMBERS):
[Performance / uptime / scale / speed. The quantified proof.]

Tweet 10 (TEAM CREDIT):
[Named names. The 2 people who cracked the hardest part.]

Tweet 11 (WHAT'S NEXT):
[Where this goes. 6 months out.]

Tweet 12 (CTA + LINK IN REPLY):
[Single ask. Try it / DM / waitlist.]

Reply tweet:
[Link]
```

## Worked example (AI-era build-reveal)

```
Tweet 1:

90 seconds. No humans. No shoot day.

[Video embedded]
```

## Variants

### Matt Epstein "AI launch video shock" variant
Compress to 4-5 tweets. Lead with "I made this launch video with [N] prompts. Creatives are cooked." Embed video. Drop frameworks. Close with "(steal it)".

### Devin "benchmark reveal" variant
Lead with a benchmark number vs SOTA. "Devin resolves 13.86% of issues end-to-end, far exceeding the previous state-of-the-art of 1.96%." Then process. Then video.

### "We replaced [expensive thing] with [cheap thing]" variant
Frame as cost/time arbitrage. "We replaced a $200K video production stack with $1,400 in API calls. Here's how."

## Pre-ship checklist

- [ ] Tweet 1 has a specific absurd number (6 prompts / 90 seconds / $340)
- [ ] Tweets 2-3 set stakes — why this wasn't done before
- [ ] Build steps (5-7) include at least ONE failure and recovery (earns trust)
- [ ] Video embedded at tweet 8 (mid-thread reveal — NOT tweet 1, NOT last tweet)
- [ ] Numbers tweet has 3+ specific metrics
- [ ] Team credit names real people
- [ ] Single CTA at the end
- [ ] Link in reply, not main thread
- [ ] No em dashes
- [ ] Thread works even if reader only sees tweets 1 and 8 (hook + reveal)
