# Quickstart

You have a launch coming up. Here's the fastest path through this repo.

---

## Step 1 (1 min) — Pick your archetype

Open the [decision tree in CHEATSHEET.md](CHEATSHEET.md#quick-decision-tree). Pick ONE of 6 archetypes. Don't blend.

---

## Step 2 (1 min) — Pick your length

| Archetype | Default |
|---|---|
| YC single-tweet | Single tweet + reply with link |
| Origin thread | 8-12 tweets OR long LinkedIn post |
| Teardown / reveal | 10-15 tweets |
| Fundraise | Long post (preferred 2025+) OR 5-8 tweet thread |
| Lead-magnet | Single tweet |
| Agency amplification | Single tweet OR 2-3 tweet mini-thread |

---

## Step 3 (1 min) — Read 1 corpus example

Open [corpus/INDEX.md](corpus/INDEX.md). Find a launch that matches yours. Read the verbatim post + the pattern analysis.

Best ones to read first:

- New product, no fundraise yet → [Crunched](corpus/01-crunched.md), [Trava](corpus/07-trava.md)
- Fundraise announcement → [Lovable $200M](corpus/14-lovable-series-a-origin.md), [Deel $300M](corpus/16-deel-origin-pivot.md)
- Free tool → service funnel → [Icon (rosetta stone)](corpus/11-icon-kennan-davison.md)
- Demo-first AI launch → [AI-era pack](corpus/13-ai-era-demos.md)
- YC dev-tool → [YC HN classics](corpus/12-yc-hn-classics.md)

---

## Step 4 (2 min) — Draft using the prompt

Open [PROMPT.md](PROMPT.md). Copy **Prompt 1 — The Master Launch Post Generator**.

Paste into Claude or ChatGPT. Fill in your product. Get a draft in 60 seconds.

---

## Step 5 (1 min) — Audit before ship

Run your draft through **Prompt 5 — The Pre-Ship Auditor** (also in [PROMPT.md](PROMPT.md)). 12-point checklist. Outputs a fixed version.

---

## Then ship

Apply the [8 hard rules](CHEATSHEET.md#the-8-hard-rules):
- Founder account
- Tuesday / Wednesday / Thursday at 9am Pacific
- Account warmed 3-5 days prior
- 2-hour engagement sprint after publishing
- Link in reply, never main post

---

## If your launch is bigger than one post

Use **Prompt 4 — The Multi-Format Multiplier**. Turns ONE launch into 4 distinct posts across the launch week (day-of, day 1 origin, day 2 LinkedIn, day 7 milestone).
